Computing & Software Development - Course Content

Visual Design and User Experience

This module is one of the six other modules that come with this course. It focuses mostly on how to create websites, how to design them and make them more appealing and easy to use for clients and users. The languages learned in this class are HTML, CSS and Javascript in the first year. The interpreter is Visual Studio Code.

Software Development

This is the main module of this course and it consists of backend software development, designing and testing programs in different languages. The programming language learned in first year is Python, being taught from the beginning, followed by Java in the next years.

Computer Architecture

This interestingly-named module focuses on how computers are programmed to function. It helps the students gain a better understanding of what processes happen in a computer system to make it run. We learn specifically about components such as the Central Processing Unit(CPU) and the Random Access Memory(RAM).

Discrete Mathematics

Discrete Mathematics is the study of mathematical structures that are fundamentally discrete rather than continuous. For example, the study of countable or distinct mathematical structures. Things such as graphs and logical statements.

Business Information Studies

Software engineers and developers generally solve problems for companies from many different industries. In order to come up with solutions to, often corporate, problems, the individual must know about how companies and business work. They must be able to coordinate and fully understand the roles of different functions within a company. The Business Information Studies teaches students about how businesses work and the overall industries that demand software developers and engineers.

Critical Skills Development

Critical skills are necessary in most of the contemporary industries. Therefore this course is required to help students develop those much needed abilities. Examples of such skills include: Communication, Time Management, General Management and Teamwork Skills.
